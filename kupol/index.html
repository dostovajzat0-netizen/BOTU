The ISS Dome (Window to the World)

⸻

The dome is not just a viewing window, but a unique instrument that is of value to the entire Earth. Benefits for the World Environment and Climate: Astronauts in the Cupola take thousands of photographs of Earth's surface. These images help scientists monitor climate change, melting glaciers, deforestation, ocean pollution, and the atmosphere. Natural Disasters: Images from the Cupola enable quicker detection of wildfires, floods, and hurricanes, allowing emergency services on Earth to respond more rapidly and save lives. Uniting Humanity: Viewing the planet without borders fosters a sense of a shared home among people. This perspective encourages cooperation over conflict and promotes thinking oriented toward the planet’s future. Science and Art: Earth imagery from the Cupola has inspired artists, writers, environmentalists, and researchers. It helps people feel a deeper connection to both space and Earth.
